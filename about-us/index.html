About Us

Unimax Global Shipping

Unimax Global Shipping Provides a wide range of logistics services to provide you the freedom to focus on your core business, We deliever domestically and internationally:

  • Receipt of goods into a warehouse
  • Quality control
  • Vertical carousel storage
  • Order receipt
  • Order picking/packing
  • Order consolidation/dispatch
  • Delivery
  • Re-order of stock lines
  • Stocktaking
  • Ticketing and labelling

With a solution built from these offerings, or including custom built services, we provide our customers with some unique business benefits:

  • Stability and reliability
  • Commitment to investment in innovation and technology
  • Integrated services based on United States Post’s extensive mail network
  • Item tracking using CourierPost’s Track & Trace technology
  • Ability to deliver to high density areas quickly and efficiently
  • National coverage, including rural delivery areas
  • Access to United States's largest integrated transport network
